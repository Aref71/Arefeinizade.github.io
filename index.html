About me

Aref Einizade

I received my M.Sc. degree in Electrical Engineering (with a focus on Bioelectric Engineering) from the Sharif University of Technology, Tehran, Iran, in 2018. I also got my Ph.D. in 2023 in Electrical Engineering from the Sharif University of Technology by developing Graph Signal Processing (GSP) and Graph Machine Learning (GML) algorithms and their applications in biomedical (mostly the brain) data processing under the supervision of Prof. Sepideh Hajipour Sardouie.

Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at LTCI, Télécom Paris, Institut Polytechnique de Paris (IP-Paris), France, working on the intersection of GSP and Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) under the supervision of Prof. Jhony Giraldo and Prof. Fragkiskos D. Malliaros. My research interests are GSP, GML, image processing, matrix and tensor decompositions, machine learning, and deep learning algorithms.
