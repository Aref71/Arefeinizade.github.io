About me

Aref Einizade

Paris, French

I received my B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from the Shahid Beheshti University of Technology, Tehran, Iran, in 2015 and my M.Sc. degree in Electrical Engineering (with a focus on Bioelectric Engineering) from the Sharif University of Technology, Tehran, Iran, in 2018. I also got my Ph.D. in 2023 in Electrical Engineering from the Sharif University of Technology by developing Graph Signal Processing (GSP) and Graph Machine Learning (GML) algorithms and their applications in biomedical (mostly the brain) data processing.

Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at LTCI, Télécom Paris, Institut Polytechnique de Paris (IP-Paris), France, working on the intersection of GSP and Graph Neural Networks (GNNs). My research interests are GSP, GML, image processing, matrix and tensor decompositions, machine learning, and deep learning algorithms.
